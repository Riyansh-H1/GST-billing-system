About The Project

SmartGST keeps local GST billing simple.

SmartGST is a frontend GST billing system built for small businesses, learners and local shop billing practice. It helps manage products, create invoices, calculate GST and keep saved invoice records using browser localStorage.

1

Product Records

Store Items

Add product name, price and GST percentage before preparing invoices.
2

GST Billing

Auto Total

Calculate subtotal, CGST, SGST and final bill amount from invoice rows.
3

Local History

Saved Bills

Keep invoice history in the browser so testing and practice stay quick.

Purpose

Why this software is useful

This project gives a small business owner or student a clean place to practice daily GST billing tasks without database setup. The pages are connected through simple HTML, Bootstrap, CSS and JavaScript.

Since the data is stored locally in the browser, it is best suited for project demos, learning, offline practice and single-device billing tests.
